    Registration Policy


  1. Each accepted paper needs at least one *Regular Early bird Registration. Otherwise, the paper cannot be included in KST-2022 conference proceedings. Please register and upload the evidences via provided e-mail link. The printed receipt of the conference fee will be issued at the time of the conference.
  2. To be eligible for publication in IEEEXplore, an author of a paper is required to present his/her paper. IEEE reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (i.e. removal from IEEE Xplore), if the paper is not presented at the conference.
  3. Member of IEEE/ECTI must declare a valid membership status (such as, a copy of member card or other membership evidences upon registration).
  4. The acceptance of the final submission will be acknowledged via email to the designated corresponding author.
